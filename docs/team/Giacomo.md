Giacomo was born and raised in Lima, Peru. He moved to the San Francisco Bay Area in early 2017 to kickstart his career in software engineering. After an initial year of training, Giacomo landed his first developer job at Scality, a software-defined storage business. During that time, he flourished his skills and interest in cloud infrastructure. This later allowed him to climb into two large companies: Apple & Google, where he worked building the global-scale deployment infrastructure of Apple Maps, and the on-prem distribution of the Google Kubernetes Engine.

Notwithstanding, Giacomo found throughout these years that his true passion was to work on more consumer-impactful work. This aspiration drove him to join AMI, an early-stage startup were his worked in a closer relationship to end-users and shipped software across the whole stack, from mobile apps to cloud infrastructure. This experience shaped Giacomo into a well grounded generalist, empowering him to start a new role at Twitch. Here he’s part of the team that ships software to enable reliable streaming at massive scale.
